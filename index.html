Kitten in the City

Audiobook ebook paperback

A Fantastic adventure with a cute main character that teaches invaluable life lessons. This story has it all, and it's a perfect story for every kid! From the renowned children's book author Charlotte Williams comes a foxy and captivating adventure story for kids, with a message of utmost importance - the value of true friendship and unconditional love... Well-known actor and narrator Ryan Wichert (Netflix actor of Queen's Gambit) makes Kitten in the City an exceptional audiobook, which will warm every listener.

About Ryan

Ryan is a British / German stage and screen actor who trained at the Drama Centre London. He's known for the Netflix series Das Damengambit (2020), dir. by Scott Frank, the BFI produced short comedy To Leech (2013), opposite Jason Watkins and Julian Rhind-Tutt and the coming-of-age feature Abschussfahrt (2015) (Constantin Films) where he played the British thug 'Bret'.
